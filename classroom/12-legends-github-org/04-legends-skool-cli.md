## Legends Skool CLI

> Skool operations from the agent desk: draft, stage, publish once, verify what landed.

Legends Skool CLI is a private operating layer for Skool work with Codex, Claude, and terminal-based workflows.

It helps with activity briefs, Obsidian drafting, review staging, confirmed publishing, classroom updates, video inventory, transcript workflows, and receipts for important changes.

## Best For

- Reading what is happening across Skool groups you can access.
- Drafting Skool posts and classroom updates in Obsidian.
- Staging posts for review before publishing.
- Publishing one confirmed update at a time.
- Verifying what landed and keeping receipts.
- Live-session video inventory, downloads, transcripts, and evidence workflows.
