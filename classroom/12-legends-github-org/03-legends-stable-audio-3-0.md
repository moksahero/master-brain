## Legends Stable Audio 3.0

> Describe the sound, generate unique tracks, and build longer mixes without looping the same song forever.

Legends Stable Audio 3.0 is a private skill suite for working with Stable Audio 3 Medium locally.

Tell your agent what kind of music you want, let it help with setup and generation, then use the finished MP3s for videos, study music, ambience, editing sessions, or longer background beds.

It is not locked to one genre or one rigid preset system. Ask for the sound in plain language. For longer sessions, it can generate multiple tracks in the same lane and blend them into one longer mix.

## Best For

- Local music generation with Stable Audio 3.
- Instrumental background music for videos, studying, ambience, and editing.
- Longer MP3 mixes made from multiple unique tracks.
- Sound effects, samples, intro/outro ideas, and ambient textures.
- Plain-language style requests instead of rigid preset picking.
- Cleaner transitions when tracks are blended together.
- Agent-assisted workflows for Codex, Claude, Gemini, and similar tools.
