# X (Twitter) Growth Engine

> **High-engagement thread creator.** Creates multiple hook variations and high-quality posts from a simple topic, then publishes directly to X.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/6b832c29adb4469ba1b9e6f31953609f8f159970487146d5b1fe59cd9d14b3e2.png)

**What it does:**

Generates high-quality posts with hook variations.

Sends final posts to n8n → Publishes to X.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/b58dcd978e5a44c68a1a6f649081dbac456caa2d91614845bec821ab9971ca84-md.png)

**⚙️ Setup Steps:**

1. Upload `v0-xtwitter-content-creator-app.zip` to[ v0.dev](http://v0.dev) chat.
2. Add your n8n Webhook URL inside v0.
3. Upload `X Twitter Content Creator.json` into n8n.
4. Connect X API credentials.
