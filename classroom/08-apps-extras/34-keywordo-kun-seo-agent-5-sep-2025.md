# Keywordo-kun SEO Agent (5 Sep 2025)

> **The GEO-Targeting Specialist.** A dedicated AI agent designed for advanced local keyword research and competitor analysis. Comes with its own custom chat interface.

Keywordo-kun isn't just a workflow; it's an interactive AI agent. We have built a custom interface (using v0) that lets you chat with your SEO data to find GEO-specific opportunities that standard tools miss.

---

**🚀 What It Does:**

**🌍 GEO-Research:** Finds keywords specific to local regions/cities.

**🤖 AI Mode:** Analyzes search intent and "AI Overview" opportunities.

**⚔️ Competitor Spy:** Breaks down competitor rankings in specific locations.

![Keywordo-kun image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/95f259fd513448e4aa8223de5f3dcd3ab24cf3b8375b4581b4ab87732aa36e11-md.png)

**⚙️ Setup Instructions:**

1. **The Interface:** Import `Keywordo-kun Chat Interface.zip` into v0.dev.
2. **The Brain:** Import the JSON workflows into n8n.
3. **Connect:** Copy your n8n Webhook URL and paste it into the v0 interface settings.
4. **Tools:** Ensure all "Keywordo-kun Tools" nodes are connected in your n8n canvas.
