# `What's in v8?`

Rankenstein v8 improves on previous versions with several key upgrades:

**Campaign-Based Input:** Starts from a high-level goal using a simple **Lovable.dev** form.

**Deeper Research:** Integrates more data sources, including **Perplexity** and analysis of **Google's AI Overview**.

**Enhanced Image Generation:** Uses **Imagen 3.0** to create relevant article images.

**Instant Notifications:** Sends you an email with the finished article in both `.docx` and `.html` formats.

---

# `Troubleshooting`

**The PDF Guides are Your First Stop.** 99% of setup questions are answered in detail within the provided guides.

**Check All API Keys.** The most common errors come from incorrect credentials. Carefully re-check every connection.

**Workflow Updates:** This is an advanced workflow. Future updates to n8n or the connected APIs may require you to troubleshoot and modify nodes.
