# Keyword Research v1 (29 Sep. 2025)

Most tools just give you a spreadsheet. This workflow acts as an SEO consultant. It automatically scans your target website, identifies ranked and suggested keywords, and uses AI to build a complete execution plan.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/b9036f6d133c439e958422e22a400edbd51b4c8618f8485f98e3f1763ddc36d5.png)

**🚀 What It Does:**

**🔎 Automated Analysis:** Scans any URL to find current rankings.

**🧠 AI Strategy:** Uses DataForSEO + AI to filter for "winning" keywords.

**📊 Visual Reporting:** Compiles everything into a beautiful, client-ready [HTML report](https://pub-6e4b77b888bc4422963711caabeb1953.r2.dev/cc97635.html).
