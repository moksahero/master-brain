# The Business Intelligence Engine

# `Strategy Before Tactics.`

Now that you've seen the visual power of AI, let's build your foundation. This workflow performs deep keyword research to give you a "Master Context" for all your future marketing.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/80471fb9d8cf4c9f9cd12687c69e24b6dcf9381736ea4135b3a14d98de512c45-md.png)

**The Workflow:**

1. **Input:** You submit your URL.
2. **Process:** AI Agents analyze your business model, competitors, and market gaps.
3. **Output:** A comprehensive Business Analysis Report (Doc/PDF) + Ranked Keyword Sheets.

> **💡 Strategy:** Run this ONCE. Then, feed the "Business Data" into every content agent you build later.
