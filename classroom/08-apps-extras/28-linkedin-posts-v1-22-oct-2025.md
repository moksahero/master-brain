# LinkedIn Posts v1 (22 Oct 2025)

> This system researches trending topics, generates 3 unique post options with custom AI images, and auto-publishes to your profile.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/b728296d8bc44376beb452defd1a4d1bc9a6dea71cb14ab09b8666cb7d254a1f-md.png)

**Type a Topic → Get 3 Posts → Publish.** This is your personal LinkedIn ghostwriter. It handles the entire creative process—from research to scheduling—managed entirely from a simple Google Sheet.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/2deb7c4508eb43db88f3fa47859bfca752f2f213ac884986b255210042e190f8-md.png)

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/a47b6c94f7364d5598786154db4499ec2b370bad899340219787ca66c81a0bf6-md.png)

**🚀 What It Does:**

**🔎 Trend Research:** AI researches what is currently trending on LinkedIn for your topic.

**✍️ 3 Unique Angles:** Writes 3 distinct post options (Story, Contrarian, or Listicle) with perfect formatting.

**🎨 AI Images:** Generates custom visuals for each post automatically.

**⚡ Auto-Publish:** Posts directly to your profile via API when you approve.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/519834e0b60f4eb9b86da4480ba502fa37ca52fdb1534689a02cdfd108d4926e.png)

**⚙️ Setup Instructions:**

1. **The Control Center:** Make a copy of the attached Google Sheet.
2. **The Engine:** Import the JSON workflow into n8n.
3. **Connect:** Link your LinkedIn API credentials in n8n.
4. **Tutorial:** Watch the full video guide below for the API setup.
