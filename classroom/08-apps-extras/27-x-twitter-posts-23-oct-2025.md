# X (Twitter) Posts (23 Oct 2025)

> **High-Engagement Tweets on Autopilot.** Dominate the feed without the burnout. This system researches trending X topics, writes 3 high-impact post variations with custom images, and schedules them directly from Google Sheets.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/e3281319de51465f870e99247c55cab0a415e0b052374f9f9256d6a5a1a1cccf-md.png)

**1️⃣ Type a Topic → 2️⃣ Get 3 Posts → 3️⃣ Publish.** Managing X/Twitter requires volume and speed. This workflow automates the creative heavy lifting, giving you viral-style posts and threads ready to go in seconds.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/c41809cae1c7422399f41e20f8a9a19d8276174d653d4136bd03696b82fee41d-md.png)

**🚀 What It Does:**

**🤖 Trend Research:** AI scans X/Twitter to see what angles are working.

**✍️ 3 Post Variations:** Writes 3 options (Short & Punchy, Thread-Starter, or Visual).

**🖼️ Auto-Images:** Generates custom AI art to stop the scroll.

**📤 One-Click Publish:** Approving a post in Sheets sends it live immediately.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/668e7af4a4ae4a49afb023c086f0b38cc418a1674e5c4c409a0cbacbb69bf82e.png)

**⚙️ Setup Instructions:**

1. **The Control Center:** Make a copy of [Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ER4zy2FHRanWf1nKNH3mxuVH0-w7wB_YMOanCksVV30/edit?usp=sharing).
2. **The Engine:** Import the JSON workflow into n8n.
3. **Connect:** Add your Twitter API credentials in n8n.
