# Keyword Research App

> **Global SEO Data at your fingertips.** Stop paying for expensive SEO tools. This custom app creates reports for 62 countries & 39 languages with CPC, Volume, and Trend data.

![2025-12-0718-17-23-ezgif.com-video-to-gif-converter.gif](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/00652667f0ce4adf8a26640bff051797ad05ab5bfbcf481eb44825d599041b1b.gif)

**What You Get:**

✅ **3 Keyword Types:** Related, Long-tail, Alternative.

✅ **Global Data:** 62 countries + 39 languages.

✅ **Metrics:** Search volume, CPC, competition data & trends.

✅ **Export:** PDF, CSV, or Clipboard.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/f4b82c3f266c4723a6c0c756ceb7da6841e04d01223b4ceb918b4a1c389d53a6-md.png)

**How It Works:**

1. Enter your keyword.
2. Pick country/language.
3. Get 10-50 results → Sort, filter, export.
