# Brand & Marketing Brief  (13 Oct. 2025)

> **The Foundation.** AI cannot write good content if it doesn't know who you are. This workflow scans your URL and generates a professional Brand Strategy Brief, saving the data for all future automations.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/ec3917a6e177408f90870f5e01b1680a70563ee95cb645bea4c5365027a1b311-md.png)

**Define Your Brand DNA.** Before running any content generators, you must run this workflow. It turns a simple URL input into a clean, professional brand brief automatically.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/a1141318644845e9b24dc7896b86eb3cbc73e5f23a01499b976749df8e7f177d-md.png)

**🚀 What It Does:**

**📥 Input:** Accepts your website URL.

**🧠 Analysis:** AI extracts your Value Proposition, Audience, and Tone.

**💾 Memory:** Uploads this data to your **"Business Data"** table in n8n.

**📄 Output:** Generates a gorgeous strategy doc you can share with clients.
