### **🧩 Setup Guide (Quick Start)**

This workflow is divided into parts for easier navigation. Follow these steps precisely:

`Step 1: The Files `

Download the **Rankenstein v9.1 Pack** attached below. Unzip the folder.

`Step 2: Google Sheets Setup`

1. Make a copy of the **Rankenstein Master Sheet**.
2. Go to **Extensions** → **Apps Script**.
3. Paste the script provided in the download folder.
4. **Set up a Trigger:** Set it to run on "Form Submit" or "Time-driven" depending on your preference (see video).

`Step 3: n8n Configuration`

1. Import the JSON workflows into your n8n instance.
2. **Firecrawl Auth:** Ensure you have "Basic Auth" set up for Firecrawl in your credentials.
