# `What This Workflow Does`

![Rankenstein v7.1 image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/08e74c5b924e48ea82902d53df2be280/8e96b7def8204c35b7d6a10646f362fa8bfa9bc20bbc4b45bfb8e6a886774aef-md.png)

**Rankenstein v7 acts like a full content agency **with a team of AI specialists:

1. `The Research Team`**:** You give it a topic, and it automatically finds the best keywords, analyzes your top competitors, and finds verified sources.
2. `The Writing Team`**:** It takes all the research and builds a comprehensive outline, then writes a full-length, E-E-A-T rich article in a voice you can customize.
3. `The Design Team`**:** It strategically decides where images should go, generates them using AI, and gives them SEO-friendly names.
4. `The Publishing Team`**:** It takes the finished article and the images and posts them directly to your WordPress site as a perfectly formatted draft, complete with a table of contents.

# `Troubleshooting & Support`

**99% of Errors are in the Setup.** If you encounter an error, your first step is to go back to the PDF guide and carefully re-check every single API key and configuration step.

**Workflow Updates:** This workflow was built with a specific n8n version. Future n8n updates may require you to adjust nodes. Be prepared to troubleshoot.

**Need Help?** The best place for support is the community! Post a screenshot of the node that has an error in the `Ask for Help❓` section.
