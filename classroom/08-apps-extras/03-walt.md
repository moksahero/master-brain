# 🎬 Walt

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/cover-real-walt.png)

Walt is the video-production brain for any creator. It is not a video editor and it does not render footage. It is an Obsidian brain plus a thin script layer that holds the craft, your style, and your performance history in one place, so every next video starts ahead of the last one.

> **Course promise:** Clone the Walt brain, open it in Obsidian, and set your Creator Style Profile so every brief, storyboard, and caption already sounds and looks like you.

Tool-agnostic. The craft is the same whether you edit in an NLE, generate with AI, record your screen, or build videos as code. Two pillars hold it up: your **Style** (the Creator Style Profile) and your **Ranking** (the performance loop). Source-available, v0.7.0.
