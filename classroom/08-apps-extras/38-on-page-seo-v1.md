# On-Page SEO v1

# `The Complete On-Page SEO Audit.`

This is the complete system that audits your entire website's on-page SEO, analyzes every page, and generates detailed reports—all from a single form submission.

**It analyzes & fixes:**

✅ Meta titles & descriptions

✅ Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS)

✅ Image alt tags & broken links

✅ Spelling errors & content quality

> **The Output:** All results populate automatically in Google Sheets with one-click access to full audit reports.
