# ✍️ Blog Prompts

> Pair these prompts with `/blog` to scope a strategy, write a post that passes the quality gates, fact-check every stat, and turn one article into a week of content.

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/chat-pl-blog.gif)

*Scope topics, draft, score, and fact-check before anything goes live.*

---

### Strategy before writing

`/blog strategy` scopes the niche and surfaces topics worth your time. Run it once per content area, then feed its picks into `/blog write`.

`/blog write` runs research, outline, draft, schema, and citation checks in one pass. `/blog analyze` scores the result on a 100-point rubric so you publish with proof, not vibes. Deep training lives in [✍️ Claude Blog](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/b5886e72?md=d36ed305e9dd4826852fe483df73fec8).

## Prompts

**Scope your blog and find topics:**

```text
/blog strategy <your niche>

Context: we sell <product> to <audience>. Find the topics where we can realistically rank and that map to a buying decision. Give me 10 ranked title ideas with the intent behind each.
```

**Write a full post from a topic:**

```text
/blog write <specific topic or working title>

Audience: <who this is for>. Voice: <plain / expert / friendly>. Make it answer-first, include sourced statistics, and add the internal links and schema automatically.
```

**Score an existing draft and tell me what to fix:**

```text
/blog analyze <path-to-draft.md>

Give me the 0 to 100 score, the three biggest point-losers, and the exact edits to push this over 90. Be specific. Quote the lines that need changing.
```

**Fact-check every claim before publishing:**

```text
/blog factcheck <path-to-draft.md>

Fetch every cited source and tell me which stats are exact-match, paraphrased, or not found. Flag anything I should remove or re-source before this goes live.
```

**Turn one post into a week of content:**

```text
/blog repurpose <path-to-published-post.md>

Repurpose this into a LinkedIn post, an X thread, a short email, and a YouTube description. Keep the core argument. Adapt the hook and length per channel.
```

---

- `/blog strategy <niche>` first. It tells you what's worth writing.
- `/blog write <topic>` produces a researched, schema'd, internally linked draft in one pass.
- `/blog analyze` scores it 0 to 100. `/blog factcheck` proves the stats are real.
- `/blog repurpose` spins one post into a week of channels.
