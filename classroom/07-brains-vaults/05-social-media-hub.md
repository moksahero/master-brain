# 💬 Social Media Hub

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/20fc142266924e2d8f2c0951fe5895cf0db14774044e4bd9ac8dada4e059acbc-md.png)

> **Social Media Hub** is the ranking brain for social. It is a source-available Obsidian vault plus a thin script layer that keeps current, dated, source-cited best practices for the seven discovery platforms and turns them into platform-native content, a calendar, and a performance loop. It is a Brain, not a prompt folder. You clone the repo and open the vault.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/0dd43e1c8bcc42b6989eba2f443bf2b94819a4e4b5e9440699282e76aa9eeca1.png)

**What this Brain does:** Covers YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, Pinterest, and Reddit on three ranking layers each: reach, social SEO, and citation by AI answer engines. Every claim carries a source URL, a retrieval date, and a monthly recheck. It repurposes one input into platform-native posts, builds a cadence calendar, scores a draft before you publish, and reports per-platform freshness. It refuses unsourced claims and never auto-posts; decisions route through an Approval Queue.
