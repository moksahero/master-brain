# 📈 Marketing Brain

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/cover-real-marketing-brain.png)

Marketing Brain is the research engine. It runs a 6-step [DataForSEO](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/2afc71bf?md=af828c1dd05c40aeb37ff7fe01730f13) pipeline into an Obsidian strategy vault, then synthesizes a source-cited ULTIMATE BEAST Plan you can hand a client. It is a Brain, not a `/command`. You clone the repo and open the vault. Your skills read from it.
