# 💰 Ads

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/cover-real-ads-brain.png)

> 🎬 **Watch:** [Claude Code just replaced your ad agency](https://youtu.be/Kf7ejOtl5KU)

Ads Brain is the operating brain for paid media. It is a source-available Obsidian vault template plus a thin script layer that turns your campaign exports into sourced recommendations, a weekly report, and a clear next action. It is a Brain, not a `/command`. You clone the repo and open the vault.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/d7a93faaa8534fce948d3b2c0023a77be4efeb506e8e431b91de858fe5c2b71b.png)

> **What this Brain does:** Takes raw platform exports (Google, Meta, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Microsoft, Apple), summarizes the signals, and writes approval-first deliverables. It never edits a live ad account.
> 
> **Feeds:** [📣 Claude Ads](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/b5886e72?md=166ec4ea11f343648e30d0c3d471245c) the weekly signal and the account memory it reports on.
