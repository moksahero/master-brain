# 🧠 Brains & Brainstein

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/cover-lvl-07.png)

A brain gives your work memory and strategy. Each one is a source-available Obsidian vault plus a thin script layer that turns your tool exports into a sourced client memory your skills can read. You clone the repo and open the vault. This classroom groups the four brains we ship plus the generator that builds new ones, for license holders and AI Marketing Hub Pro members.

> **What you get:** Strategy you can open, check, and reuse. Marketing Brain orchestrates the research. Website Brain captures any site. Brainstein scaffolds and scores a brain for any domain we do not ship.

## What's inside

- [🧠 Marketing Brain](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=888db513f2b24f4d85320fe80ee88bbb) The research engine. Runs a 6-step DataForSEO pipeline into a strategy vault, then synthesizes a source-cited 30/60/90-day plan you can hand a client.

  ![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/85fb0599126a46d0af85d0450843512f998846eda78d4d519795a1dda00f163e.png)
- [🏪 Local SEO Brain](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=22902679826f428295b66ca4141160bd) The operating brain for ranking local businesses. Ships seeded with 59 chapters of doctrine and 37 prompts, then ingests GBP, citation, review, and geo-grid exports into a per-client memory with a sourced Health Scorecard. Uses a DataForSEO key.

  ![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/fe9a46193d9e475ca292359c887dc169dd61d61599f842eb9dec5e22cea8d815.png)
- [💰 Ads Brain](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=9166b414941a45cb851448ef0c5ee7ac) The operating brain for paid media. Turns raw platform exports into sourced recommendations, a weekly report, and a clear next action. It never edits a live ad account.

  ![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/6de87a8acb7042ce98286a44929992b2f45ee56e628c40178fc0c7ee5daf7de4.png)
- [🌐 Website Brain](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=1dc04a82c97547d1aa66353d805793b5) The capture brain. Point it at any site and it crawls the whole thing with Firecrawl, then builds a self-contained brain: one note per page, an image library, a generation-ready brand-tokens.json, and a wikilink graph that mirrors the site.

  ![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/f56215261abe4eb6a1f61fa1f574412e90c092961147403aba990a6b275c7794.png)
- [🧠 Brainstein](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=96488a5da2a74801bc83da74aafa756e) The generator that builds you a brain for any domain we do not cover. Write a short YAML spec and it scaffolds a complete second-brain repo: vault, agent skill pack, source ledger, demo, tests, and a 33-item audit. Advanced and optional.

  ![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/f680f7145b1648d588014b617386acd5ef0e3fd364014e5d87e0ba19d71977dd.png)
