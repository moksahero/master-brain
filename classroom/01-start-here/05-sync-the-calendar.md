# 🗓️ Sync the Calendar

> **What you'll be able to do:** Get the weekly live builds onto your own calendar in under a minute.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/8cd0e5fa8133418fa45a2809c14561fe9d358f59ddb049b487490ec12f40fa60.png)

**Live builds every week. **We build workflows. I answer questions. You debug yours, live. The feed moves fast. Live is where the real work happens.

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/45d6a06c43fd42dcaf26cff43d5d163566da7ff0245a459faf230c0e0bbe3633.png)

**What you're building:** the weekly build on your own calendar, with a reminder. **Before you start:** be logged in on web (the calendar tab is easiest there). **Steps:**

1. Go to the **Calendar** tab at the top of the community.
2. Click the next **Weekly Workflow Build** event.
3. Hit **Add to Calendar** (Google, Apple, or Outlook).

**You're done when:** the next build shows up on your own calendar.
