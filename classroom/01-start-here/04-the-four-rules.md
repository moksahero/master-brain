# 📜 The Four Rules

> **What you'll be able to do:** Operate in the room the way it's meant to work. High signal, zero spam.

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/c1-four-rules.gif)

This place stays valuable because of four rules. They're short. They're not

1. 🤝 **Give value.** Figure something out? Post the workflow. The room gives back what you put in.
2. 📢 **No self-promotion.** No service pitches, no spam DMs. You're here to grow, not to sell.
3. 🔎 **Search first.** Most answers are already posted. Search before you ask.
4. 🔒 **Confidentiality.** What's shared in this room stays in this room.

> **✅ React to this lesson.** If you've already learned something useful, post it as a workflow (rule #1). Giving value early is the fastest way to get known.
