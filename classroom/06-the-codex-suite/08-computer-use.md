# Computer Use

**Computer Use (Codex Drives Your Apps)**

Computer use lets Codex operate any app on your Mac, clicking and typing in the real interface, not just files and code. It uses its own cursor in the background, so it keeps working while you keep using your computer, and it only touches the apps you explicitly allow. Point it at the task that has you bouncing between five apps (think updating spreadsheets, moving data between tools). *Mac today, Windows soon.*

> **Think of it this way:** A second pair of hands on your keyboard, working in your apps while yours stay free.
