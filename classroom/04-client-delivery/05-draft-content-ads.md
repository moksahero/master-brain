# ✍️ Draft: Content & Ads

> Turn the 30-day plan into real deliverables: optimized pages, posts, and campaigns.

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/chat-cf-draft.gif)

*Ask how to make the work and Claude pulls the draft commands for pages, posts, and ads.*

---

## Content & pages (the usual core)

```text
/blog write <topic from the plan>
```

Drafts a post built to rank and get cited by AI. Save to `content/`. For a page that exists but won't rank, optimize instead of rewrite:

```text
/seo content <url>
```

Multiplying a winner? Build the cluster:

```text
/seo cluster <core keyword>     +     /blog cluster
```

![image.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/49ff1f2d656742a68e4b871cd8c0e543/ca2b3bcac390446d93c4d6dbc554e0763e03405f21394c0095c21f7b37edd603.png)

## Structure & AI visibility (do it as you draft, not after)

- `/seo schema <url>` so Google understands the page.
- `/seo geo <url>` + `/blog geo` so ChatGPT and AI Overviews cite it.

## Ads (when there's a page that already converts)

Only run paid at a page that ranks *and* converts. Then:

```text
/ads audit          (fix what's leaking first)
/ads                (build/optimize the campaign)
```

Paid amplifies a winner. It doesn't fix a weak page.

---

## Keep it in the folder

Everything saves into the client folder: `content/`, plus updated pages. One client, one place.

**You're done when:** the top 1-3 plan items exist as real drafts in `content/`.

> 💡 Don't draft everything at once. Ship the top item, get it reviewed (next step), then move down the list. Momentum beats a giant unreviewed pile.

> ✅ Draft one deliverable from your plan (`/blog write` or `/seo content`) and post what you made. Wins and what needed fixing.

- Work from the plan, top item first. `/blog write`, `/seo content`, `/seo cluster`.
- Do schema + GEO as you draft, not after.
- Ads only amplify a page that already ranks and converts.
