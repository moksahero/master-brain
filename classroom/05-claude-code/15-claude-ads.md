# 📣 Claude Ads

Claude Ads turns [Claude Code](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=f579fae5e640447f90c73fb4ee8ed75f) into a paid-media audit room. A manual audit of one Google Ads account takes 4 to 6 hours of senior PPC time. Claude Ads does the same job in 10 to 15 minutes. You run one command, parallel agents check 8 platforms, and you get one Ads Health Score from 0 to 100 with the top fixes ranked.

It covers Google, Meta, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Microsoft, Apple, and Amazon. 250+ checks behind one `/ads` command, plus AI creative generation, attribution, and server-side tracking deep dives. Every finding has an ID, an impact, an owner, and an ETA. So it's a task list, not a wall of data.

> Audit any ad account in 10 to 15 minutes, read the report like a senior PPC lead, fix what matters first, and generate on-brand creative. All from the `/ads` command.

The full `/ads audit` hits all 8 at once and weights each by budget share. But each platform also has its own command, so you can go deep on one.

**Google** (`/ads google`): Search, PMax, AI Max, Demand Gen, CTV, YouTube. The most coverage.

**Meta** (`/ads meta`): Pixel and CAPI health, creative fatigue, structure, audience, the Andromeda stack.

**YouTube** (`/ads youtube`): skippable in-stream, Shorts, Demand Gen, Connected TV.

**LinkedIn** (`/ads linkedin`): B2B targeting, Lead Gen forms, budget.

**TikTok** (`/ads tiktok`): creative-first scoring, Events API, bidding, structure.

**Microsoft** (`/ads microsoft`): search, structure, plus Google import validation.

**Apple** (`/ads apple`): Apple Ads placements, Custom Product Pages, AdAttributionKit.

**Amazon** (`/ads amazon`): Sponsored Products, Brands, Display, ACOS and TACOS.
