# ✍️ Claude Blog

Claude Blog turns [Claude Code](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=f579fae5e640447f90c73fb4ee8ed75f) into a full blog engine. You run one command. It researches, drafts, adds schema, links internally, makes a hero image, and fact-checks every stat. The output is built to do two jobs at once: rank on Google and get cited by AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews.

It's 30 sub-skills and 5 agents behind one `/blog` command. A 5-gate Delivery Contract grades every draft to 90 out of 100 before you ever see it. You're never the first reviewer. The gates are.

> **S**hip a high-quality post with one command. Then scale into clusters, AI-citation tuning, and other languages. All from `/blog`.

**Write a post** — `/blog write <topic>` — Fresh post from a topic. Runs the full pipeline and the 5 gates. Your default.

**Build a cluster** — `/blog cluster plan <seed>` then `/blog cluster execute <seed>` — You want to own a whole subject, not one keyword. Plan maps the hub and spokes. Execute writes the set.

**Tune for AI citation (GEO)** — `/blog geo <file>` — You want ChatGPT and Perplexity to quote a specific post. Audit only. For Google + AI together, use `/blog rewrite`.

**Pass the quality bar** — (automatic) re-check with `/blog analyze <file>` — Every `/blog write` runs the 5-gate contract for you. Run `analyze` to re-score an old post or one you edited.

**Go multilingual** — `/blog multilingual <topic> --languages <codes>` — Same post in several languages, culturally adapted, with hreflang. One command.
