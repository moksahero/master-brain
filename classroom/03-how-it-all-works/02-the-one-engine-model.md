# 🧱 The One-Engine Model

> Hold the entire Hub in your head as one picture, so it stops feeling like a pile of separate products.

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/AI-Marketing-Hub/classroom-assets@cfc5a1e99148d256bff96912f16ee28322523f56/eco-c3.gif)

There is **one engine: **[**Claude Code**](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=f579fae5e640447f90c73fb4ee8ed75f) (or Codex, if that's your CLI). Everything else is something that plugs into it or feeds it.

---

- [**Skills**](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=c4ccd34fe8754432a44496f89985917e) plug *into* the engine and do the work. `/seo`, `/blog`, `/ads`.
- [**Brains**](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=267ca6db814347ebb54fc1e1de033998) feed the engine memory and strategy. The skills read from them.
- [**Codex**](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/708c1575?md=d1ba646701774675a4fa5032e757dfb7) is the same lineup for a different engine.

---

People feel lost because they think each product is a separate thing to learn. It isn't. **You learn one engine once.** Then adding a skill is like adding an app to your phone. The phone didn't change. You just gave it one more ability.

So when you see "[Claude SEO](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/b5886e72?md=0e87e1b9a1ea4e0ba7047bf57960939f), [Claude Blog](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/b5886e72?md=d36ed305e9dd4826852fe483df73fec8), [Marketing Brain](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/c1d53bcf?md=888db513f2b24f4d85320fe80ee88bbb), [SEO OS](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/62de05f5?md=3562bd77965742c7811d4c22c9063f01), [Codex SEO](https://www.skool.com/ai-marketing-hub-pro/classroom/ded6ca5c?md=0c6b4c4b3c914c91a1667dc82883390c)..." don't read it as five things to master. Read it as: one engine, plus the pieces you'll plug in *when a job needs them*.

---

- One engine (Claude Code or Codex). Everything else plugs in.
- Four kinds of piece: Skills do work, Brains give memory, Apps visualize, Codex mirrors it.
- You learn the engine once. Skills are just abilities you add per job.
