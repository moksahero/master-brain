# YouTube Brain Research Plan

Status: required before researched or market-ready maturity.

## Source Targets

- Official product, platform, API, vendor, regulator, or standards-body docs for YouTube creator growth and strategy: the recommendation system and discovery, audience retention and average view duration, packaging (titles, thumbnails, click-through rate), Shorts versus long-form, publishing cadence, monetization (YouTube Partner Program and RPM), analytics interpretation, and community..
- Primary datasets or exports the buyer already controls.
- Dated practitioner sources only as supporting evidence.
- Market evidence for buyer pain, recurring workflows, and paid demand.

## Buyer Questions

- Who pays for this brain?
- What raw materials do they already have?
- What output saves time or reduces risk?
- What weekly or monthly workflow brings them back?
- What should the brain refuse to do?

## Official-Source Checklist

- [ ] Source URL recorded.
- [ ] Retrieval date recorded.
- [ ] Version, API version, or policy date recorded when available.
- [ ] Sunset/deprecation notes recorded when available.
- [ ] Confidence level assigned.
- [ ] Contradictions or gaps recorded.

## Safety Questions

- Private channel analytics, audience, revenue, or comment data in raw inputs
- Stale recommendation or monetization policy leading to wrong advice
- Overconfident growth projections from incomplete or short channel history
- Rapid platform policy shifts (demonetization rules, harmful content definitions)
- Generated reports leaking local filesystem paths or third-party tool API drift
