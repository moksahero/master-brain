# Market Research

Status: researched as of 2026-06-23.

## Buyer Hypothesis

YouTube creators, channel managers, editors, and content strategists who need repeatable, source-cited decisions on what to make, how to package it, and how to grow and monetize a channel.

## Demand Signals

- Posting cadence correlates strongly with growth: across 5,000,000 channels, 12+ uploads per month generated 53% more views and 66% more subscribers than 1-3 per month (vidIQ, 2025-07-31). Creators want help producing more without lowering quality.
- Packaging is the highest-leverage lever: thumbnail A/B testing has been used over 15,000,000 times since 2023, and title testing rolled out globally around 2025-12-09 (YouTube Official Blog, 2025-09-16). Demand for packaging decisions is proven by adoption.
- Retention is the bottleneck: field data shows average retention near 23.7% and 55%+ of viewers lost in the first 60 seconds (Retention Rabbit, 2025). Creators repeatedly seek hook and pacing fixes.
- Monetization confusion is widespread: the two-tier YPP, the 45% versus 55% split, RPM versus CPM, and the 8-minute mid-roll threshold are frequently misunderstood (YouTube Help, 2024-2026). Sourced clarity reduces costly mistakes.
