# Connected TV and Shorts Ads

Connected TV (CTV) is YouTube's fastest-growing ad surface and the center of its
2025 to
2026 advertising story, while Shorts ads expand the short-form inventory. The
living-room
screen, long a pure awareness surface, is becoming a direct-response one, which
changes what
advertisers can do there. Because this area moves fast, re-verify launch details
before
advising; see [[Source Intake and Refresh]]. The format and campaign mechanics
are in
[[Ad Formats]] and [[Ad Campaign Types]].

## CTV as the new center

At Brandcast 2025 on 2025-05-14, coinciding with YouTube's 20th anniversary,
YouTube
announced an immersive edge-to-edge Masthead on CTV for the homepage and
Shoppable CTV that
lets viewers browse and engage with products from the big screen. YouTube is
positioned as
the central living-room surface, and has been the number one streaming platform
in the US
for three straight years per industry reporting.

## Shoppable CTV

Shoppable CTV ads rolled out broadly on 2026-01-23 after roughly seven months of
testing.
They pull product images (500 by 500 pixels or larger) and data from Google
Merchant Center
feeds, display as clickable carousels with QR codes, and run through Performance
Max, Demand
Gen, and Display & Video 360 on the smart-TV app. Reported results: QR codes
lifted
conversions more than 100 percent, and TV grew from under 0.5 percent to about
1.5 percent
of Demand Gen impressions. This makes the QR code the bridge from the big screen
to a phone
purchase; measure it via [[Ad Measurement]].

## Non-skippable reach for CTV

Google launched non-skippable Video Reach Campaigns for CTV globally on
2026-03-03,
supporting 6-second, 15-second, and 30-second CTV-only non-skippable ads, with
AI optimizing
the mix across lengths. This is the reach buy tuned for the television context;
see
[[Ad Bidding and Budget]].

## Shorts ads

Shorts ads keep expanding as a multi-format reach and views surface, delivered
across Demand
Gen, video views campaigns, and reach campaigns. A Shorts ad view counts at 10
seconds, the
full ad, a call-to-action click, or an engagement. Shorts ads sit beside the
organic
[[Shorts]] feed, so creative that respects the vertical, sound-on, fast-hook
format performs
best.

## How to apply

- Treat CTV as a reach-plus-shoppable surface now, not just awareness; use the
  30-second non-skippable CTV format and shoppable carousels where relevant.
- Connect a Merchant Center feed to unlock shoppable CTV.
- Design Shorts ads vertical and hook-first, like organic [[Shorts]].
- Re-check rollout availability by market; these features ship region by region.
