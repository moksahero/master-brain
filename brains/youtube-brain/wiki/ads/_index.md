# Advertising

The YouTube advertising (paid) pillar, run through Google Ads. See [[index]] and
[[YouTube Brain Home]].

- [[Ad Formats]]: skippable and non-skippable in-stream, bumper, in-feed,
  Shorts, Masthead.
- [[Ad Campaign Types]]: Demand Gen (the Video Action Campaign migration), video
  reach, video views.
- [[Ad Bidding and Budget]]: CPV vs CPM vs conversion bidding, and what a "view"
  means.
- [[Ad Targeting]]: audience segments, restricted categories, EU consent,
  made-for-kids limits.
- [[Ad Measurement]]: paid vs earned vs view-through, Unique Reach, Brand Lift.
- [[Connected TV and Shorts Ads]]: CTV as the growth surface, shoppable
  carousels, Shorts ads.
- [[Brand Suitability and Inventory Controls]]: inventory modes, content and
  placement exclusions, where ads are allowed to run.
- [[Masthead and Reservation Buying]]: the premium Home-feed reach format and how
  reservations are bought.

This is the paid surface, distinct from creator-growth (organic) and the
developer API.
