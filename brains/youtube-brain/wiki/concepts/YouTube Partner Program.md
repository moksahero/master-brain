# YouTube Partner Program

The YouTube Partner Program (YPP) is the gateway to monetization, and since 2023
it runs in
two tiers. A lower tier unlocks fan funding earlier, and a full tier unlocks ad
and YouTube
Premium revenue sharing. Knowing exactly which threshold you are chasing, and
that Shorts
views and watch hours are alternative paths, prevents wasted effort. These
figures are fast
moving; verify against the official help page before advising anyone. See
[[Source Intake and Refresh]].

## The two tiers

Lower fan funding tier:

- 500 subscribers, plus
- 3 valid public uploads in the last 90 days, plus
- 3,000 valid public watch hours in the last 12 months, or 3 million valid
  public Shorts views in the last 90 days.
- Unlocks memberships, Super Chat, Super Stickers, Super Thanks, gifts, and
  Shopping.

Full monetization tier:

- 1,000 subscribers, plus
- 4,000 valid public watch hours in the last 12 months, or 10 million valid
  public Shorts views in the last 90 days.
- Adds ad revenue sharing and a share of YouTube Premium revenue.

Valid public watch hours exclude private, unlisted, and deleted videos, ad
campaigns, and
Shorts. Other requirements include living in a YPP country, no active Community
Guidelines
strikes, two step verification, and an AdSense account.

## What unlocks at each tier

- Fan funding ([[Revenue Streams]]): [[Channel Memberships]] at up to six levels
  and [[Supers and Gifted Memberships|Supers]] in live chat and on videos, all at
  a 70 percent creator share.
- Ads: the watch page 55 percent split on long form and the 45 percent Shorts
  pool; see [[Mid-Roll Ads]] and [[Shorts Monetization]].
- Shopping, open to YPP creators with 500 or more subscribers; see
  [[YouTube Shopping and Product Tagging]].

## Staying eligible

Monetization is governed by the [[Advertiser-Friendly Guidelines]] and the
channel
monetization policies. The 2025-07-15 policy renamed repetitious content to
inauthentic
content, targeting mass produced or templated channels; reused content can still
monetize
with significant original commentary. See [[Inauthentic Mass-Produced Content]]
under
antipatterns once you build it, and [[Chasing Views Over Satisfaction]].

## How to apply

- Identify the single threshold you are closest to and plan content to hit it.
- Decide whether watch hours or Shorts views is your realistic path; they are
  alternatives, not both required.
- Re-check the exact numbers before any monetization recommendation; they
  change.
