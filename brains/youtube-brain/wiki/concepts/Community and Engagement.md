# Community and Engagement

Community features manufacture the comments, returning viewers, and session time
that the
[[Recommendation System]] rewards, and they open fan funding
[[Revenue Streams]]. Engagement
is not vanity; it is the satisfaction and loyalty signal made visible. A creator
who treats
the channel as a two way relationship, not a broadcast, builds the returning
audience that
compounds growth. These features also keep viewers on the channel between
uploads, smoothing
the gaps in [[Publishing Cadence]].

## The surfaces

- Posts: text, polls, quizzes, images, GIFs, and video, available broadly since
  2024-06-04. Posts can surface on the home and Subscriptions feeds, and image
  posts can appear in the Shorts feed.
- Communities: a fan driven space launched at Made On YouTube 2024 where fans
  post and reply to each other and the creator. To start, only subscribers can
  post, and creators control who can post and the behavior guidelines.
- Comments: pinning and hearting comments shapes the conversation and rewards
  your best fans.
- Live and Premieres: real time formats that drive Super Chat and Super Stickers
  revenue and concentrated engagement; see [[Supers and Gifted Memberships]].
  Premieres add a countdown and live chat, then become regular videos.
- Memberships: up to six tiers of perks that deepen loyalty; see
  [[Channel Memberships]] and [[Revenue Streams]].
- Subscriptions: the subscribe action and the bell are the base of the returning
  audience; see [[Subscriptions and Subscribers]].

## Why engagement compounds

Comments, returning viewers, and longer sessions are all signals the system
uses, and they
are downstream of relationship, not tricks. Genuine replies, community polls
that shape
upcoming videos, and consistent presence turn casual viewers into a base that
clicks every
upload. This is the honest opposite of [[Bought Engagement and Sub4Sub]], which
manufactures
hollow numbers the system can detect and discount.

## Engagement and the funnel

Community surfaces are also conversion tools. A pinned comment linking a Short
to its long
form companion is one of the strongest click paths, and a community post can
drive a launch.
See [[Shorts-to-Long-Form Funnel]].

## How to apply

- Reply to early comments on every upload; the first hour of conversation
  matters.
- Use posts and polls to involve the audience in upcoming content.
- Pin a comment that routes viewers to the next step, a related video or
  playlist.
- Track returning viewers and watch-time from subscribers in
  [[Studio Analytics]].
- Never buy engagement; see [[Bought Engagement and Sub4Sub]].
