# Shorts

Shorts are YouTube's short form vertical videos, served in a dedicated feed that
runs its
own recommendation model. The Shorts feed weights the swipe versus watch
decision in the
first 1 to 3 seconds, replays and loops, and shares, and it weights metadata
less than long
form. For most creators Shorts are a discovery engine: cheap to produce, high in
reach, low
in revenue per view, and best used to feed the long form catalog. See
[[Shorts-to-Long-Form Funnel]] and [[Shorts Monetization]].

## Two changes that reshaped Shorts

- Length: since 2024-10-15, standard channels can upload Shorts up to 3 minutes;
  anything square or vertical up to 3 minutes is categorized as a Short, while
  16:9 stays long form. Most music is usable for up to 90 seconds in a 3 minute
  Short.
- View counting: since 2025-03-31, a Shorts view counts on every play or replay
  with no minimum watch time, aligning with TikTok and Reels. The older
  sustained metric is renamed engaged views and still governs monetization and
  [[YouTube Partner Program]] eligibility.

## Retention is the swipe decision

On the Shorts feed, the decisive metric is "Viewed vs swiped away," the
percentage of times
viewers watched rather than scrolled past. The first second is the entire hook.
Loops reward
a clean cut from the end back to the beginning, which can push average
percentage viewed
above a single play. Target a high APV, often 60 percent or more. See
[[Audience Retention]]
and [[Watch Time and AVD]].

## Strengths and limits

- Strength: reach. Shorts surface to new viewers fast and can grow subscribers
  quickly.
- Limit: revenue. Shorts RPM is far below long form; see
  [[Shorts Monetization]].
- Limit: loyalty. A Shorts view is shallow; converting it to a long form view or
  subscription is the real win. See [[Shorts-to-Long-Form Funnel]].

## How to apply

- Lead with a 1 second hook; assume the viewer decides instantly.
- Design for the loop; make the end flow back into the start.
- Use Shorts to trail a long form video, with a pinned comment or a specific
  verbal call to action.
- Do not expect Shorts to pay like long form; treat them as top of funnel
  discovery feeding [[Publishing Cadence]].
